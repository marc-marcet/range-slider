Range Slider

Example 1: Basic Range

HTML:

Example 2: Price Range with Callback

Current range: $100 - $500

HTML:

HTML:

Example 4: Weight Range with Suffix

HTML:
<div data-range-slider data-min="0" data-max="100" data-label-suffix="kg"></div>
